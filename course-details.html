Overview

The AWS Certified AI Practitioner training is designed to help individuals develop a foundational understanding of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) services offered by AWS. It prepares participants to pass the AWS Certified AI Practitioner exam by covering key concepts, best practices, and core services used by AWS to implement AI and ML solutions.

Course Objectives

  • Learn how to use AWS AI and ML services such as SageMaker, Recognition, and Lex.
  • Understand AWS solutions for AI/ML workflows, model building, and deployment.
  • Develop basic AI and ML models using AWS services.
  • Prepare for the AWS Certified AI Practitioner exam.
  • Understand the fundamental concepts of AI and ML.

Curriculum

✶ Core AI Terminology: Understanding AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, and Natural Language Processing.

✶ Types of Predictions: Classification, Regression, and Anomaly Detection.

✶ Data Types for AI Models: Structured, Unstructured, and Semi-Structured Data.

✶ Learning Patterns: Introduction to Supervised, Unsupervised, and Reinforcement Learning.
